# Matin de Boulangerie — Four Composers' Scratchpads

*Four parallel AI agents, each given the same song contract, each independently
planning and composing the piece. Each uses a different feature profile for
feedback (the agents don't know which profile they have — and neither does the
listener). Below are their unedited planning documents.*

## Observations

Four independent composers, same brief, same story — and look at the convergences
and divergences:

**All four agree on:**
- F-C-F arpeggiated ostinato for the morning routine
- Ascending stepwise clarinet motif for the "first glance"
- D minor excursion at m.37-40 for the moment of shyness
- Ab major for the emotional peak
- The primary motif returning in the piano at the end
- Fadd9 as the final unresolved chord

**Where they diverge:**
- Track 01: motif as F-G-A-Bb-A (concert pitch), settling back down
- Track 02: motif as C5-D5-E5-F5-G5, reaching higher — the most ambitious leap
- Track 03: same as 02 but with explicit resolution back to F5
- Track 04: motif as F4-G4-A4-Bb4, staying in a lower, warmer register
- Track 04 uniquely uses Dm/C# - Dm/C chromatic bass descent in the D minor section
- Track 03 uses Eb7 as a chromatic approach chord in Section III — most harmonically adventurous
- Track 02 deploys Em7b5 and Bbm chord borrowing — jazz-influenced touches

*Each composer heard the same story and found their own voice in it.*
