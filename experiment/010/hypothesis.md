# Experiment 010: New Premise, New Instrumentation

**Date:** 2026-03-17
**Status:** Pre-registered

## Hypothesis

The clarinet writing weakness found in experiment 009 is instrument-specific, not a
general limitation. A different instrumentation and premise — chosen by the composer
agent — will produce better results across the board.

## Design

Same 2×2 factorial as experiment 009:

| | v1 (PDMX) | v3 (Canonical) |
|---|-----------|----------------|
| **exp5** (revise based on feedback) | v1-exp5 | v3-exp5 |
| **exp9** (feedback as suggestions) | v1-exp9 | v3-exp9 |

**Changed variables:**
- Vibe: "A chess game between old friends in a park — the stakes are a bottle of wine"
- Instrumentation: Cello + Piano (testing whether the LLM writes cello better than clarinet)
- Time signature: 3/4 (waltz-adjacent, different from 6/8)

## Predictions

1. All scores will be higher than experiment 009 (no clarinet weakness)
2. exp5 workflow will again beat exp9 (pattern holds)
3. The cello will be written more idiomatically than the clarinet was
4. v1-exp5 will win again (the most validated approach)
